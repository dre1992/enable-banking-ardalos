# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: {{ "now" | date: "%B %d, %Y" }}_

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to **ardalos_monthly_reconcile** ("the Application"), a personal,
non-commercial software tool operated solely by **Andreas Seficha** ("the Operator")
for the Operator's own use. The Application has no other users.

The Application accesses bank account information of the Operator via PSD2
Open Banking APIs (Account Information Services) provided by Enable Banking Oy
acting as the licensed Account Information Service Provider (AISP).

## 2. Data Controller

The Operator is the sole data controller for any personal data processed by
the Application. For the purposes of GDPR data-subject requests, the Operator
is also the sole data subject.

**Contact:** aseficha@gmail.com

## 3. Data Processed

The Application retrieves the following categories of data, strictly limited
to bank accounts the Operator owns and has explicitly authorised:

- Account identifiers (IBAN, account name)
- Account balances
- Transaction records (date, amount, currency, counterparty, description)

No data is collected from any other person.

## 4. Purpose of Processing

The data is used exclusively for the Operator's personal financial automation,
record-keeping, and analysis. No automated decision-making, profiling, or
processing for third-party purposes is performed.

## 5. Storage and Retention

Retrieved data is stored locally on the Operator's own computing devices.
The data is not transmitted to, stored on, or processed by any third-party
service other than the licensed AISP (Enable Banking) and the account-servicing
bank itself, both of which act as separate, independently-regulated controllers
for the data they hold.

The Operator retains data for as long as it remains useful for personal
record-keeping and may delete it at any time.

## 6. Sharing

No personal data processed by the Application is shared with any third party.

## 7. Legal Basis

Processing is based on the Operator's own consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR) and
on legitimate interest in managing the Operator's own financial records
(Article 6(1)(f) GDPR).

## 8. Data Subject Rights

As the sole data subject, the Operator may at any time exercise rights of
access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection by
acting on the locally-stored data directly. Active data-sharing consents
granted via Enable Banking can be reviewed and withdrawn at
[https://enablebanking.com/data-sharing-consents/](https://enablebanking.com/data-sharing-consents/).

## 9. Security

Data is protected by the security controls of the Operator's personal devices
and the authentication mechanisms (Strong Customer Authentication) enforced
by the bank and the AISP.

## 10. Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the Application or in
applicable regulation. The current version is always available at the
URL where this document is published.

## 11. Contact

Questions or requests relating to this policy may be directed to:
**aseficha@gmail.com**
